## Testing Date/Time Edge Cases

### Zero Offsets
NOW with zero offset: 2026-02-20 15:08:29
MONTHDELTA with zero offset: 2026-02-20 15:08:29

### Large Positive Offsets
NOW +365 days (1 year ahead): 2027-02-20
NOW +730 days (2 years ahead): 2028-02-20
MONTHDELTA +24 months (2 years ahead): 2028-02-20
MONTHDELTA +60 months (5 years ahead): 2031-02

### Large Negative Offsets
NOW -365 days (1 year ago): 2025-02-20
NOW -1095 days (3 years ago): 2023-02-21
MONTHDELTA -24 months (2 years ago): 2024-02-20
MONTHDELTA -36 months (3 years ago): 2023-02

### Various Date Format Strings
ISO 8601 basic: 20260220
ISO 8601 extended: 2026-02-20T15:08:29
ISO 8601 with timezone: 2026-02-20T15:08:29+00:00
Unix timestamp style: 20260220150829
Human readable: Friday, February 20, 2026
Date only: 2026-02-20
Time only: 15:08:29
Year and month: 2026-02
Month and day: 02-20
Custom format 1: 20/02/2026 15:08
Custom format 2: 2026.02.20.15.08.29
Custom format 3: February 20, 2026 at 03:08 PM

### MONTHDELTA with Various Formats
Month delta ISO: 2026-08-20T15:08:29+01:00
Month delta basic: 20251120
Month delta date only: 2027-02-20
Month delta first of month: 2026-03-01T00:00:00Z

### Combined Edge Cases
Far future NOW: 2036-02-18 (10 years ahead)
Far past NOW: 2016-02-23 (10 years ago)
Far future MONTHDELTA: 2036-02 (10 years ahead)
Far past MONTHDELTA: 2016-02 (10 years ago)
